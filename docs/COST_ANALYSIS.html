AI Sales Assistant — Cost summary

Deepgram (speech) + Gemini or OpenAI (text) + servers + optional post-call analysis. Figures are planning examples — replace with your vendor invoices.

Rates used in examples: Deepgram ~$0.009/streamed minute · Gemini-style text ~$0.004/suggestion (live coaching) · Post-call: $0.30/M input + $2.50/M output tokens. Multilingual speech and English-first knowledge base; optional Hindi translation for search adds small extra Gemini use.
